Recipes

Choosing the right food for game night is important to keep guests fueled up for fun! We like to have plenty of finger foods available and napkins plentiful! Some good ideas would be veggie trays, cheese and meat trays, charcuterie boards. Below you will find a few recipes that we have made and guests have loved at some of our Game nights.

Crock Pot Cheese Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb sausage
  • 16 oz picante sauce
  • 16 oz salsa
  • 2 Velveeta cheese bricks
  • 2 bags Tostitos scoops

Directions:

  1. Brown ground beef and sausage in a skillet until done then add to crock pot on high heat
  2. Cut up Velveeta bricks into small pieces (this helps the cheese melt faster)
  3. Add salsa and picante sauce
  4. Stir occasionally until cheese is melted and it is mixed thoroughly then turn crockpot to keep warm.
  5. Serve and enjoy

Flourless Peanut Butter Banana Chocolate Chip Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • Â¼ cup almond flour
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Line a pan with parchment paper
  3. In a large bowl mash the bananas.
  4. Add to it the Peanut Butter, almond flour, maple syrup, egg, cinnamon, baking soda and salt.
  5. Mix until just combined then fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Transfer batter to pan and top with more chocolate chips.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Allow these to cool then serve
